Spider Cookies

This is a fun Halloween sweet treat that is easy enough for kids to help make. I modified an idea I found online to be less expensive and easier to put together.

Scary Spider Cookies

Ingredients

  • spiders
  • cookies
  • powdered sugar

Steps

  1. catch 12 spiders
  2. make 12 sugar cookies
  3. throw away the spiders
  4. make peanut butter ball spiders instead
